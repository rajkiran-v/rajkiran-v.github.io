With a strong emphasis on accessibility and scalability, my research has actively contributed to public health interventions, rural outreach, and population-level cardiovascular risk tracking. Using ARTSENSÂ® technologies, we have conducted high-throughput vascular health screenings in Kerala and Tamil Nadu, enabling community-level detection of early vascular changes. We are also contributing to multi-center vascular phenotyping studies to understand ethnic and lifestyle-driven vascular variability in the Indian population. Targeted efforts include the development of low-cost, non-invasive BP and stiffness monitors for pregnant women, with the goal of improving maternal cardiovascular health.
